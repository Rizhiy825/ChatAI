As soon as you switch to material mode during a construction, the selection window opens.
You can use the selection window to decide whether surfaces are to be provided with a new material and which edging is to be applied to the item.
Select one or more items in your design to expand the selection window.
Items:
Tick the box to make all items used in the design, except for drawer insides and components, selectable for the material selection.
Components:
Tick the box to make the components used in the design selectable for the material selection.
Drawer Insides:
Tick the box to make the internal parts of the drawers used in the design selectable for the material selection.
