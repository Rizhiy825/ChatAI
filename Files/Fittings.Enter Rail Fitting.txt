To insert a rail/tube fitting into your construction, you need an item on which you can mount the fitting.
Select the desired rail fitting and drag it into your construction.
In the template window, you have the option of making adjustments to the fitting.

Supplier:
Indicate the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the drop-down selection.
Click on the X symbol in the line to delete the supplier entered in the line from your list.
Article:
Enter the article number of the fitting.
X Position:
Specify the position of the rail on the X-axis.
To change the axis for determining the position, click on the right arrow in the line.
Y Position:
Specify the position of the rail on the Y-axis.
To change the axis for determining the position, click on the right arrow in the line.
>
Angle:
Specify the angle at which the rail should be rotated.
Extension Left:
Specify how far the rail is to be extended, over the volume, on the left side.
If you have added a pocket for e.g. a rail handle to the rail fitting when creating it, this will be added automatically when extending it.
Extension Right:
Specify how far the rail is to be extended, over the volume, on the right side.
If you have added a pocket for e.g. a rail handle to the rail fitting when creating it, this will be added automatically when extending it.
Additional Info:
Specify additional information that you want to automatically assign to the fitting.
