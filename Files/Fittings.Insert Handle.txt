To add a handle to your construction, you need a door or a drawer front, which you can put the handle on.
Select the desired handle and drag it into your construction.
In the template window you have the possibility to make adjustments to the fitting.

Supplier:
Specify the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the dropdown selection.
Delete the supplier entered in the line from your list by clicking on the X symbol in the line.
Article:
Specify the article number of the fitting.
Horizontal Distance:
SmartWOP shows you how large the horizontal distance of the handle to the outer edge of the workpiece is.
Vertical Distance:
SmartWOP shows you how large the vertical distance of the handle to the outer edge of the workpiece is.
Angle:
Specify at what angle the handle should be.
Horizontal Align:
Specify how the handle, in the horizontal, should be aligned.
Vertical Align:
Specify how the handle, in the vertical, should be aligned.
Absolute Position:
Under the vertical alignment, select the base surface and specify the absolute position of the handle from the base line.
