Click on the arrow to the left of the panel name to open the cutting plan for this material.
Name/Description:
SmartWOP shows you the component designations of the individual workpieces in the panel planner overview.
The panel thickness used is shown in brackets next to the material name.
Number:
SmartWOP shows you the component number of the individual workpieces in the panel planner overview.
The item numbers are also displayed on the item in the panel itself.
Result Length/Width:
SmartWOP shows you the finished dimensions of the individual workpieces in the panel planner overview.
Amount:
SmartWOP shows you the quantity of the individual workpieces in the overview of the panel planner.
You can select and mark workpieces by clicking on them in the overview.
The panel dimensions displayed depend on the stored panel dimensions of the material.
