Supplier:
Specify the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the dropdown selection.
Delete the supplier entered in the line from your list by clicking on the X symbol in the line.
Article:
Enter the article number of the leg.
Icon:
Click on the dots in the line to open the selection menu.
Select an image stored on your computer for the icon of the fitting.
Import:
You have the possibility to import already defined feet as .VRML file.
Fittings imported as .VRML file, already contain the fitting's drilling pattern.
Position:
Open the drop-down menu to position the .VRML imported fittings.
Position:
Open the drop-down menu to move the imported fitting in X/Y/Z axis.
Rotation>:
Open the drop-down menu to rotate the imported fitting in X/Y/Z axis.
The length and the width are determined by the .VRML file and are only displayed after the import.
Length:
Enter the length of the leg.
Width:
Enter the width of the leg.
Cylindric:
Set the hook when the leg is Cylindrical.
Remove the hook when the leg is rectangular.
Minimal Height:
Specify the minimum height of the leg.
Maximal Height:
Specify the maximum height of the leg.
Additional Info:
Specify additional information that you want to assign to the fitting automatically.

Switch to machining mode while creating the fitting to add or remove machining to the fitting.
When you have added the appropriate hole pattern to the fitting, switch back to the fitting mode.
Confirm your self-defined fitting by clicking on the orange arrow in the drop-down menu of the fitting selection bar..
