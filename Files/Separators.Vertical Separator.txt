Drag the vertical separator in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.
Absolute Position:
Determine the space of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between left and right base outside line.
Height:
SmartWOP will show the height of the separator.
