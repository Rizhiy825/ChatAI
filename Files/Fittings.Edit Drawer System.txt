Icon:
Click on the dots in the line to open the selection menu.
Select an image stored on your computer for the icon of the fitting.
Supplier:
Indicate the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the drop-down selection.
Click on the X symbol in the line to delete the supplier entered in the line from your list.
Article:
Enter the item number of the drawer system.
Space Bottom Indent:
Enter the lower distance of the frame to the calculating base.
Panel Generation:
In the drop-down menu of the workpiece setting you have further settings to output the drawer interior parts to fit the fitting.
Default Depth:
Specify the standard depth of the drawer system.
Material:
Specify the material from which the drawer interiors are to be made.
Use the arrow on the right of the line to select a material from your material database.
Rail Width:
Specify the installation clearance of the fitting.
The installation clearance is calculated from each side.
Limit Distance to Bottom:
Check the box if you want to limit your frame height from the center of the pull-out.
Distance to Bottom:
Specify how deep the frame should run from the center of the pull-out.
Remember that if you want to determine the distance to bottom via the limit, the space bottom indent should be set to 0.
Additional Info:
Specify additional information that you want to automatically assign to the fitting.

Switch to machining mode while machining the fitting to add or remove machining to the fitting.
When you have added the appropriate drilling pattern to the fitting, switch back to the fitting mode.
Confirm your machining using the orange arrow in the drop-down menu of the fitting selection bar.
