To insert a leg fitting into your construction, you need a horizontal plate on which you can mount the leg fitting.
Select the desired adjustable leg fitting and drag it into your construction.
In the template window you have the possibility to make adjustments to the fitting.

Supplier:
Specify the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the dropdown selection.
Delete the supplier entered in the line from your list by clicking on the X symbol in the line.
Article:
Specify the article number of the fitting.
Count:
Specify the number of legs required.
Rows:
Select from the drop-down menu on the right whether the leveling feet are required Front, Rear or Front and Rear.
Offsets:
Specify the distances of the legs from the outer edge of the base plate, in each case for left, right, front and back.
Row Distance:
Specify the distances between the legs.
On the right of the line, by clicking on the icon, you have the possibility to specify whether the distances of the legs should be given in mm or in division.
Rotation Angle:
In the dropdown menu you have the possibility to adjust the rotation angle of each leg.
Height:
The height of the foot fitting is already assigned to the fittings and is only displayed in the template.
