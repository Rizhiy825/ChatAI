Icon:
Click on the dots in the line to open the selection menu.
Select an image stored on your computer for the icon of the fitting.
Supplier:
Specify the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the dropdown selection.
Delete the supplier entered in the line from your list by clicking on the X symbol in the line.
Article.:
Enter the article number of the angle bracket.
Additional Info:
Specify additional information that you want to automatically assign to the fitting.

Switch to machining mode while editing the fitting to add or remove machining to the fitting.
The visualization of the angle fitting is only created once machining has been added to the fitting.
The separator in the angle fitting editing mode help you to create the drilling pattern so that you can easily draw the angle fitting into your construction.
When you have added the appropriate drilling pattern to the fitting, switch back to the fitting mode.
Confirm your machining by clicking on the orange arrow in the drop-down menu of the fitting selection bar.
