In the base setting window of "Roof Window" you have several settings to enter.
Window Width
: Enter the width of the window.
Window Height
: Enter the height of the window.
Window Depth
: Enter the depth of the window.
Total depth
: Enter the total installation depth.
Thickness
: Enter the Thickness of the material.
Roof Angle
: Enter the angle of the roof.
Angle Left
: Enter the angle of the left panel.
Angle Right
: Enter the angle of the right panel.
Setup Frame
Enter the desired frame dimensions (depth/width) for the window and wall connection.
