Drag the frontal traverse in the base.
You can drag the traverse by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the panel.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your panel.

Name
: Enter a name for your traverse.
Absolute Position
: Determine the space of your panel to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between front or back base outside line.
Width
: Enter the width of the traverse.
Thickness
: Enter the thickness of the traverse.
Type
: Choose what execution you want for your traverse, select between two panels, only front panel or only back panel.
Top Distance
: Enter a distance for the upper traverse.
Bottom Distance
: Enter a Distance for the lower traverse.
Skewed Edges
: If you have angled panels which fit to other panels you can trim or extend the panel if needed.
Pick Point
: If you add a component or split a panel by WoodWOP you can choose which part of your panel will be needed.
