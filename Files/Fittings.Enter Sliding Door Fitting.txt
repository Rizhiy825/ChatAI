To fit a sliding door system into your construction, you need a sliding door on which you can mount the fitting.
Select the sliding door fitting and pull it into your construction.
In the template window, you have the option of making adjustments to the fitting.

Supplier:
Indicate the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the drop-down selection.
Click on the X symbol in the line to delete the supplier entered in the line from your list.
Article:
Enter the article number of the fitting.
Distance:
Specify the distance from the center of the sliding door carriage to the outer edge of the sliding door.
The distance of the sliding door carriage machining is taken from the center of the cup bore.
Accessories:
In the drop-down menu, SmartWOP shows you which accessories are required for the retracted sliding door system.
Additional Info:
Specify additional information that you want to automatically assign to the fitting.
