You need a room on which you can raise room elements.
Drag the window to one side of the room to add it to the room.
After opening the window, you can drag it to the desired position on the room wall using the coordinate system.
The template window of the room elements does not open until it has been drawn onto the room.
In the template window you have the option of customising the window.

Width:
Specify the width of the window.
Height:
Specify the height of the window.
Position X:
Specify the position of the window, in X-axis of the room side.
The coordinate system in the construction shows you from which direction the position X is taken.
You also have the option of entering the position X directly in the dimension line in the drawing.
Position Z:
Specify the position of the window, in Z-axis of the room side.
The coordinate system in the construction shows you from which direction the position Z is taken.
You also have the option of entering the position Z directly in the dimension line in the drawing.
Frame:
In the drop-down menu you have the option of adding a frame to the window and adjusting it.
Mounting Width:
Geben Sie die Breite der Fenstersprossen an.
Mountig Thickness:
Geben Sie die dicke der Fenstersprossen an.
