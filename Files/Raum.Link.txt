When you switch to room mode, the room start bar button appears in the top selection bar.
Linking is used to create a horizontal room edge.
Select a horizontal room edge in your construction.
The selected room edge is marked red.
Click on the button to divide the room edge.
When splitting the room side, a new room corner is automatically created.
Select another horizontal room edge and divide it as well.
Now select the two divided room corners via the multiple selection and link them.
