You can use the material template to select different panel materials and edge materials and add them to your items.
Name:
SmartWOP shows you the material name of the selected material.
Panels:
Select the materialpanel you want to add to your selected items.
Grain Rotation Angle:
Enter the grain angle to rotate the grain on the selected items.
Edges:
Select the edge material that you want to add to your selected items.
Edges banding:
Select how the edges are to be arranged for the selected items.
By default, the front edge goes through and the rear edge jumps in between the side edgings.
