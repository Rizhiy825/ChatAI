Drag one of the room types into the scene.
In the template window, you have the option of customising your room.

Name:
Give the room a name.
Height:
Specify the height of the room.
Width:
Specify the width of the room.
Depth:
Specify the depth of the room.
Transformation:
Specify the position of the room in the scene.
The position in X/Y/Z are measured from the centre of the room.
