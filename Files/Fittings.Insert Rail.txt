To insert a rail fitting into your construction, you need a workpiece on which you can mount the fitting.
Select the desired rail fitting and drag it into your construction.
In the template window you have the possibility to make adjustments to the fitting.

Supplier:
Specify the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the dropdown selection.
Delete the supplier entered in the line from your list by clicking on the X symbol in the line.
Article:
Specify the article number of the fitting.
X Position:
Specify the position of the rail on the X axis.
To change the axis for determining the position, click on the Right arrow in the line.
Y Position:
Specify the position of the rail on the Y axis.
To change the axis for determining the position, click on the Right arrow in the line.
Angle:
Specify the angle at which the rail should be rotated.
Extension left:
Specify how far the rail should be extended, over the volume, on the left side.
If you have added a pocket for e.g. a recessed grip to the rail when creating it, it will be added automatically when extending it.
Extension right:
Specify how far the rail should be extended, over the volume, on the right side.
If you have added a pocket for e.g. a recessed grip to the rail when creating it, it will be added automatically when extending it.
Additional Info:
Specify additional information that you want to add to the fitting on the material list.
