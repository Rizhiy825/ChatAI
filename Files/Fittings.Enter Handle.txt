To add a handle to your design, you need a door or drawer front that you can pull the handle onto.
Select the desired handle and drag it into your construction.
In the template window, you have the option of making adjustments to the fitting.

Supplier:
Specify the supplier of the fitting.
Select an already created supplier via the drop-down selection.
Click on the X symbol in the line to delete the supplier entered in the line from your list.
Article:
Enter the item number of the fitting.
Horizontal Distance:
SmartWOP shows you how large the horizontal distance of the handle to the outer edge of the handle is is.
Vertical Distance:
SmartWOP shows you what the vertical distance of the handle to the outer edge of the component is.
Angle:
Specify the angle at which the handle should be placed.
Horizontal Align:
Specify how the handle, in the horizontal, should be aligned.
Vertical Align:
Specify how the handle, in the vertical, should be aligned.
Absolute Position:
Under vertical alignment, select the base area and specify the absolute position of the handle from the base line.
