Drag the Universal Panel in the base.
You can drag the universal panel by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the panel.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your panel.

Name
: Enter a name for your Panel.
Position
: Enter the center position of the universal panel in X/Y/Z.
Rotation
: Enter the rotation of the universal panel in X/Y/Z.
Thickness
: Enter the thickness of the panel.
Orientation
: Determine the orientation of the diagonal panel z. This can be helpful to bring the panel in the desired position.
Skewed Edges
: If you have angled panels which fit to other panels you can trim or extend the panel if needed.
Pick Point
: If you add a component or split a panel by WoodWOP you can choose which part of your panel will be needed.
