When you switch to room mode, the room start bar button appears in the top selection bar.
Select a room edge in your construction.
The selected edge is marked red.
Click on the button to divide a room edge.
When dividing the edge, a new room corner is automatically created.
The edge is automatically split in the middle and the corner can be moved using the input fields on the edge.
Upright room edges cannot be split.
You can divide an edge as much as you like.
