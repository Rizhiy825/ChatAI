Separators

Insert Separators

Before you can insert Items you will need to have a base.
Insert separators in your base by drag and drop.
Remember that you work from the outside to the inside in SmartWOP.
Separators that you enter to your construction will split the base without adding panels.
You will need separators to plan more complex constructions.
If you construct panels which will be calculated on separators you can not delete the separator without deleting the panel.
If you separate panels with a separator you can link them together by selecting the panels and right clicking to choose link in the dropdownmenu.

Horizontal Separator

Drag the horizontal separator in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Absolute Position: Determine the space of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between top or bottom base outside line.

Vertical Separator

Drag the vertical separator in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Absolute Position: Determine the space of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between left and right base outside line.
Height: SmartWOP will show the height of the separator.

Frontal Separator

Drag the frontal separator in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Absolute Position: Determine the space of your Separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between front and back base outside line.

Diagonal Separator X

Drag the diagonal separator X in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Absolute Left Position: Determine the space of the left side of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between top and bottom base outside line.
Absolute Right Position: Determine the space of the right side of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between top and bottom base outside line.
Angle: Enter the desired angle for your separator. You can enter an angle of-89° to 89°.
Safety clearance: Enter a safety clearance to determine how much space you want to have between other diagonal separators.

In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Absolute Front Position: Determine the space of the front side of your separator to the base outline.
Absolute Back Position: Determine the space of the back side of your separator to the base outline.

Diagonal Separator Z

Drag the diagonal separator Z in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Absolute Left Position: Determine the space of the front side of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between front and back base outside line.
Absolute Right Position: Determine the space of the back side of your separator to the base outline. The arrow in the right side of the row allows you to switch between front and back base outside line.

Orientation: Determine the orientation of the diagonal separator Z. This can be helpful to bring the separator in the desired position.

Universal Separator

Drag the universal separator in the base.
You can drag the separator by arrows in the right place or enter the desired dimensions for the separator.
In the settings window you will find more settings to optimize your separator.

Position: Enter the centerposition of the separator in X/Y/Z.
Rotation: Enter the rotation in X/Y/Z of the universal separator.
