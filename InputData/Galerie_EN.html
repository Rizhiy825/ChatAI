Gallery

Overview Gallery

In the bottom right-hand corner of the screen, you have the option of switching to your SmartWOP gallery.
In the gallery, you can enter the folder paths of the components, projects or Wop programmes and call up or drag in the saved content directly in SmartWOP.
Constructions that are used directly from the gallery can be called up by double-clicking or dragged and dropped into the scene.
Constructions in your gallery can be moved within the gallery using drag and drop.

Select folder path

Click on the folder icon in the top right-hand corner of the screen to select the path to the desired folder.
You can select the folder in the pop-up window.
You have the option of selecting a folder in which there are several folders in order to display them directly with additional subfolders in your gallery.

Pin folder

If you have selected a folder path and displayed it in your gallery, you can pin this folder path to the gallery and access the content quickly.
The pinned folders are displayed on the left-hand side of the gallery.
To access the contents, click on the name of the pinned folder.

Create new folder

You have the option of creating folders in your gallery by clicking on the icon.
If you create a new folder in your SmartWOP gallery, it will be created in the selected path and folder.
New folders that you create in your SmartWOP gallery are also automatically created in the selected folder on your computer.
Folders, like individual elements, can be moved within the gallery using drag and drop.

Renaming in gallery

You have the option of renaming folders or elements in your gallery by clicking on the icon.
Select the folder or item from your gallery by clicking on it with the left mouse button.
Rename the selected element in the window that opens.
You can also change the extension by renaming the elements, so .swp (projects) can be converted to .swk (components).
The same renaming function is also available in reverse, so .swk (components) can be converted to .swp (projects).
As long as you have not selected an element in your gallery, the rename function is greyed out and cannot be used.

Delete out of gallery

You have the option of deleting folders or elements from your gallery by clicking on the icon.
Select the folder or item from your gallery by clicking on it with the left mouse button.
If you delete an item from your gallery, it will also be deleted from your computer.
Deleted items are moved to your computer's recycle bin.

Sort alphabetically

Click on the icon to sort your elements in the gallery alphabetically.
In sorting, the folders are sorted first and then the individual elements are sorted alphabetically.
Sorting is active when the icon is highlighted in blue.
If sorting is deactivated, the folders and elements are sorted alphabetically, but folders and elements are not separated during sorting.

Small icons

You have the option of adjusting the size of the icons in your gallery.
Activate the selection by clicking on the icon of the desired size.
The active icon size is highlighted in blue.

Medium icons

You have the option of adjusting the size of the icons in your gallery.
Activate the selection by clicking on the icon of the desired size.
The active icon size is highlighted in blue.

Big icons

You have the option of adjusting the size of the icons in your gallery.
Activate the selection by clicking on the icon of the desired size.
The active icon size is highlighted in blue.
