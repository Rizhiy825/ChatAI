Liste der Tastenkürzel

Shortcuts

In SmartWOP, you can use keyboard shortcuts to perform functions more quickly.
The respective shortcut key is displayed in SmartWOP next to the function.

Open Doors


Press the 1 key to open or close the doors in your construction.

Open Drawers


Press the 2 key to open or close the drawers in your construction.

Open Fronts


Press the 3 key to open or close all fronts in your construction.

Center View


Press the A key to centre the view on the selected item.

Categorie Machining


Press the B key to go directly to the machining mode.

Hide Fronts


Press the F key to hide the fronts.

Hide Unselected


Press the G key to display only the selected parts.

Hide Selected


Press the H key to hide the selected parts.

Show All


Press the J key to display all parts.

Link


Press the L key to link the selected items.

Categorie Materials


Press the M key to switch to material mode.

Explosion


Press the O key to activate the exploded view.

Function Measure


Press the R key to activate the tape measure function.

Show separator


Press the S key to hide the separators.

Solid Mode


Press the T key to activate or deactivate the transparency mode.

Volume selection mode


Press the V key to activate the volume selection mode.

Export Tuning


Press the X key to open Export Tuning.

Duplicate selected base

+
Press the Alt and C keys to duplicate the selected volume.

Delete selected item


Press the Del key to delete the selected item.

Subtractive selection method

+
Press the Alt key and the left mouse button to deselect individual items.

Additive selection method

+
Press the Ctrl key and the left mouse button to select individual parts.

Frame

+
Press the Shift key and the left mouse button to drag a frame.

Change view

+ bis
Press the Ctrl and 1 to 9 keys to change the view to the selected item.
