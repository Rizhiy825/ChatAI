Home bar - View

View bar

In the view bar, functions of the optics are displayed.

Rotate

Activate the button if you want to rotate the scene with the left mouse button pressed and move the scene with the right mouse button pressed.

Pan

Activate the button if you want to move the scene with the left mouse button pressed and rotate the scene with the right mouse button pressed.

Zoom

Activate the button if you want to zoom in or out of the scene while holding down the left mouse button.

Isometric 315°

Press the button to see the selected workpiece from the 315° angle. If no workpiece is selected, the entire volume is placed in the scene at the 315° angle.

Isometric 45°

Press the button to see the selected workpiece from the 45° angle. If no workpiece is selected, the entire volume is placed in the scene at a 45° angle.

Isometric 225°

Press the button to see the selected workpiece from the 225° angle. If no workpiece is selected, the entire volume is placed in the scene at the 225° angle.

Isometric 135°

Press the button to see the selected workpiece from the 135° angle. If no workpiece is selected, the entire volume is placed in the scene at the 135° angle.

View

In the submenu of the button, you have the option of displaying the workpiece or the volume from a predetermined view.
Press the button to see the selected workpiece in the desired view. If no workpiece is selected, the entire volume is placed in the scene in the desired view.

Grid

Activate the button to display the basic layer as a grid.

Boards

Activate the button to call up the workpiece list of all items in a separate window.

Show Distances

Activate the button to display distances. If the button is not activated, no dimension lines are displayed.

Show Board Numbers

Activate the button to display the component numbers in your Construction.

Price Table

Activate the button to open the price list in a separate window.

Show Estimate

Check the box to display the price of the current construction in the upper right corner of the screen.

Hide Fronts

Click the button to hide the fronts.

Hide Selected

Click on the button to hide the selected workpieces.

Hide Unselected

Click on the button to display the selected workpieces alone.

Show All

Click on the button to display all workpieces.

Hide Fittings

Click on the button to hide the fittings.

Show Separator

Click on the button to show or hide the separators.

Solid Mode

Select the 'Solid Mode' button to switch from transparency mode to material mode.
In the submenu of the button, you have the option of switching contour trains on and off in material mode.
To return to transparency mode, click the button again.
